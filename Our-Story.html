Our Story

In the bustling heart of Alta Vista, Pardo, Cebu City, Philippines, a charming flower shop named La Casa de las Flora first bloomed in 1996. Rooted in front of the prestigious Miriam College, its founder, Maria Rodriguez, poured her heart into every arrangement, reflecting its humble beginnings as a haven for nature's beauty. Over the years, La Casa de las Flora has expanded its reach, bringing unparalleled floral designs and elegance through its exquisite floral creations. We combine fine artistry and a commitment to service, thereby becoming a go-to destination for those seeking to celebrate life's special moments with the language of flowers.

La Casa De Las Flora

The Founder

La Casa de las Flora's co-founder, Larry Demeeca, closed for a few years while he trained abroad in advanced floral design. Upon his return, he established himself not only as a floral designer and event stylist in Pardo, Cebu City before co-founding La Casa de las Flora with Maria Rodriguez, shared his expertise with aspiring florists.

Larry's vision centered on the growth of the business to ensure top-notch flower service by personally overseeing every arrangement. He introduced advanced, award-winning floral trends, offering dynamic designs that are way ahead of the common flower shops. From swooping bows and luxurious ribbons to fine craftsmanship that every arrangement speaks volumes, ready to serve even the most highly discerning client.

Today, La Casa de las Flora is a well-established flower shop with branches in Pardo, Cebu City, and Carcar City. We use a wide variety of local and imported flowers, sourced both locally and internationally, and assist you in achieving the perfect arrangement.
