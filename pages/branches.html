Our Branches: The Backstory

In the vibrant heart of Cebu, Philippines, a charming flower shop named La Casa de las Flores first blossomed in 1892. Starting as a small home-based studio in Pardo, Cebu City, the shop initially bore the name Casa Florera, highlighting its roots in crafting delicate handmade floral pieces alongside fresh, real flower arrangements. As the business grew, La Casa de las Flores became known for its artistry, quality, and warm customer service. Today, the shop continues to flourish, proudly serving customers from its expanding locations — Pardo, Cebu City, Minglanilla, and Carcar City — bringing beauty to every celebration through both handcrafted blooms and authentic fresh flowers.
