About La Casa de Las Flores Flower Shop

We are La Casa de las Flores, a flower shop in Alta Vista, Pardo, Cebu City offering fresh blooms and everlasting arrangements made from materials such as crochet yarn and LEGO bricks. We take pride in creating high-quality floral pieces for any occasion, ensuring beautiful designs and excellent service whether you want natural flowers or creations that last forever.
