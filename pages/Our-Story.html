Our Story

In every culture and across every generation, humanity has searched for a language worthy of its deepest feelings. Before words can be found, before a vow can be spoken, flowers have always carried the weight of the human heart. Rooted in this profound tradition of floriography—the silent language of blooms—La Casa de las Flora was established. We exist not merely to sell arrangements, but to be the trusted messenger for those moments that define a life: the silent "I love you" shared between partners, the profound "I'm sorry" offered to a family member, or the gentle "We remember you" for a cherished memory. Through exquisite artistry and meticulous selection, we translate complex, heartfelt emotions into a tangible creation, giving form to the unspoken love that binds us all.

La Casa De Las Flora

The Founder

The genesis of La Casa de las Flora was driven by a powerful emotional commitment: to give eloquent voice to human feeling. Founder Samantha Genon, an elite designer, returned from advanced training with a singular vision for Cebu City—to ensure that every joyous celebration, every heartfelt apology, and every profound memory could be perfectly articulated through unrivaled floral artistry. To realize this ambition, Samantha Genon established the firm alongside three instrumental co-founders: Ryan Porquiado, Althea Gimena, and Robert Campos.

This dedicated leadership team ensures that the creative vision is supported by flawless execution. While the team's combined expertise covers strategic growth, stringent quality control, and large-scale event production, their shared focus remains on the client's emotional needs. They introduced award-winning trends, transforming the regional market by treating each arrangement not as a commodity, but as a crucial, delicate message sent from one heart to another.

Today, La Casa de las Flora remains a respected floral institution in Pardo and Carcar City. Their commitment is simple: to serve a highly discerning clientele by combining the freshest, most luxurious blooms with an integrity of design. They don't just arrange flowers; they translate complex human emotions into beauty, ensuring the silent language of flowers is always articulated with impeccable grace.
